W7 Night Wolves Bot

W7 Night Wolves Bot connects TikTok creator features with the W7 Night Wolves Discord community.

The integration is used for community management, creator notifications, live announcements, and content organization for the creator C7•BISHOP.

What the app does

  • Displays basic public TikTok profile information authorized by the creator.
  • Lists recent public TikTok videos authorized by the creator.
  • Sends creator content notifications and live alerts to a Discord community.
  • Helps organize the W7 Night Wolves gaming community.

What the app does not do

  • No fake engagement.
  • No automated likes, follows, views, or mass comments.
  • No spam or view manipulation.
  • No collection of TikTok passwords or private credentials.
