Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

W7 Night Wolves Bot is used to connect TikTok creator information with a Discord community.

The bot may access and store limited information needed for its features, such as TikTok username, public profile information, public video information, Discord user ID, Discord username, server roles, live alert status, ranking information, moderation logs, and bot command logs.

The bot does not collect TikTok passwords, TikTok login credentials, private messages, payment information, or sensitive personal information.

The bot does not sell user data.

Information is used only for Discord community features, TikTok content notifications, live alerts, ranking, moderation, and server organization.

Users may request removal of stored Discord-related data by contacting the W7 Night Wolves server owner or administrator.

This bot is not used for fake engagement, automated likes, automated follows, mass comments, spam, or view manipulation.
